CUSTOMER LIFETIME VALUE ANALYSIS

Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) is a key indicator of a company's revenue potential from its customer base. By predicting the net profit attributed to the entire future relationship with a customer, businesses can make informed decisions on marketing strategies, customer acquisition, and retention efforts. In my analysis, I utilized SQL for data cleaning and Power BI to accurately visualize CLV, providing valuable insights into customer behavior and profitability.
